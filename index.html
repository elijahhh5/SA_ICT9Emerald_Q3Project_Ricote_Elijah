About Rex Orange County

Rex Orange County, born Alexander O'Connor, is a British singer-songwriter known for his unique blend of indie pop, jazz, and hip-hop influences.

Early Life

Alexander spent his early life immersed in the vibrant musical culture of Surrey, England, where he honed his passion for music and began crafting his unique sound.

Success

Rex Orange County's ascent to success stemmed from his distinctive musical style, genuine lyricism, and relentless dedication, captivating audiences worldwide and solidifying his status as a celebrated artist in the contemporary music scene.
